Welcom to FC Barcelona

Club History

Founded in 1899, FC Barcelona has become one of the most prestigious football clubs in the world. Known for its rich history, the club has a legacy of success both domestically and internationally.

Famous Players

FC Barcelona has been home to some of the greatest football players in history, including Lionel Messi, Xavi Hernandez, and Andres Iniesta. Their contributions have been pivotal to the club's success.

Stadium

The Camp Nou, with a seating capacity of over 99,000, is the largest stadium in Europe. It is not only a football stadium but a symbol of Catalan culture and pride.
